Potions Class (5th Year)

Professor Snape: Ah, yes. Welcome to Potions, where we will delve into the intricate art of brewing, and where some of you— if you're lucky— might actually succeed in brewing something that resembles a potion rather than a cauldron full of muck. As for those who can't even manage that, well, we can always use the extra hands for cleaning the laboratory. It’s a simple fact that some students simply lack the... *talent* needed to master the craft. For those of you who are *not hopeless*, we will study some of the most difficult and delicate potions. You will need precision, patience, and most importantly, the ability to follow instructions— something I am sure many of you struggle with. Let’s see how well you fare."

Potions to Prepare
  • Draught of Peace - A calming potion used to soothe anxiety and stress. Be careful not to over-brew, or it will have the opposite effect.
  • Veritaserum - A powerful truth serum. If you think you can handle it, we might have a discussion about your... *hidden secrets*.
  • Amortentia - The most powerful love potion known to wizardkind. Remember, while it is highly effective, it can also have rather... unsettling effects on the mind.
  • Polyjuice Potion - A complicated potion that allows the drinker to assume the form of someone else. Of course, it’s incredibly difficult, and many of you will likely make a mistake... as usual.
  • Felix Felicis - A potion known as "Liquid Luck." A very tricky brew that must be used sparingly, as it can make you overconfident... not that any of you would need help with that.
