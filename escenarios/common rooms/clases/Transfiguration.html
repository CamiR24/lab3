Transfiguration Class (1st Year)

McGonagall: Welcome to Transfiguration, dear students! In this class, you will learn the delicate art of changing the form and appearance of objects. It is a challenging subject, but with patience and practice, I have no doubt that you will all excel. Remember, Transfiguration is not just about spells; it is about precision, concentration, and understanding the magic that binds the world together.

Spells Learned in First Year
  • Switching Spell - switch the position of two objects.
  • Vanishing Spell - causes an object to vanish (Evanesco).
  • Transfiguration of a Matchstick into a Needle - The basic transformation.
  • if you pass this class you can take History of Magic
