Defense Against the Dark Arts Class (3rd Year)

Professor Lupin: Welcome, everyone, to Defense Against the Dark Arts. I'm Professor Lupin, and I promise you, this class will be different from the others. We're not just going to study theory, we're going to practice real-life defense tactics. This subject is vital, and it can be both exciting and dangerous, but with the right preparation, you'll be able to face any threat that comes your way. No need to be frightened, just be prepared to face some of the darker aspects of magic. I trust you'll all rise to the challenge!

Spells Learned in Defense Against the Dark Arts
  • Expecto Patronum - Protective spell used to repel Dementors and other dark creatures.
  • Lumos - Used to light the tip of a wand, useful in the dark.
  • Riddikulus - Used to transform a Boggart (a shape-shifting dark creature) into something funny and harmless.
  • Expelliarmus - The Disarming Charm, used to disarm an opponent by causing their weapon or wand to fly out of their hand.
  • Stupefy - The Stunning Spell, which temporarily stuns a target, rendering them unconscious for a short period.
