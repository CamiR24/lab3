History of Magic Class (2nd Year)

Professor Binns: Ah, yes, students... Welcome to History of Magic. I am Professor Binns, and I shall be your guide through the dull and uneventful world of magical history. Do pay attention, though it is unlikely that any of you will find this subject particularly... thrilling. We shall cover the key events, such as the Founding of Hogwarts and the rise of dark wizards, but do not expect excitement, as my lessons are not known for their... er... liveliness. Now, let us move on, shall we? We shall begin with the events listed in your syllabus, which you are required to memorize for your upcoming exams. Good luck, I suppose.

Important Events in History of Magic
  • The Founding of Hogwarts - The creation of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry by Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin.
  • The Goblin Rebellions - A series of uprisings by the goblins in the wizarding world throughout the centuries.
  • The Rise of Dark Wizards - The history of dark magic and its practitioners, including the infamous Lord Voldemort.
  • The International Confederation of Wizards - The establishment of the governing body for magical communities worldwide.
  • The Salem Witch Trials - A brief overview of the magical persecution in America during the 17th century.
